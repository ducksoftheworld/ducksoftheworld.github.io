Ducks in Popular Culture: Cheers!

In Season 2, Episode 8 of the hit sitcom Cheers!, Carla the waitress (Rhea Perlman) has a baby, but keeps a stuffed duck in the stroller. Norm and Clive are looking at it thinking it is a baby.
